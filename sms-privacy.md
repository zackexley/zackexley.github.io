# SMS Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 13, 2026

This privacy policy applies to **Zack Exley Alerts**, a low-volume SMS program operated by Zack Exley.

## What information we collect

If you consent to receive text messages from Zack Exley Alerts, we may collect:

- your mobile phone number
- your name or identifying details needed to recognize you as an approved recipient
- message history needed to send, deliver, and manage the SMS service
- opt-in and opt-out records

## How we use your information

We use this information only to:

- send scheduling reminders, account notifications, and one-time verification codes
- maintain delivery records and consent records
- respond to support requests
- honor opt-out requests

## No sale or sharing for marketing

Your information is **not sold**. Your information is **not shared with third parties for marketing purposes**.

We may share limited information only with service providers needed to deliver the text messages, such as telecommunications providers and messaging platforms.

## Message frequency

Message frequency varies and is low volume.

## Your choices

You can opt out at any time by replying **STOP** to any message. For help, reply **HELP**.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, email [zack@newconsensus.com](mailto:zack@newconsensus.com).
